Place licensed street/CCTV-like demo clips here.

Supported formats:
- .mp4 video
- .webm video
- .jpg / .png / .webp image

Example:
frontend/assets/camera-feeds/olaya_fire_01.mp4

Then update the matching camera in:
backend/data/camera_scenarios.json

Example:
"media_url": "/static/assets/camera-feeds/olaya_fire_01.mp4"

Recommended clip length for hackathon demo: 5-12 seconds.
Avoid videos with readable license plates, visible injured people, copyrighted TV/news logos, or unlicensed YouTube footage.
