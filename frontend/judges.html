S.I.R.S

Smart Incident Response System
Camera-based Multi-Agent System for smart city incident response — detecting, analyzing, dispatching, rerouting, and reporting in one flow

❌ The Problem

Modern cities suffer from slow, disconnected emergency response systems that operate in isolation.

⏱️ Slow Response
Agencies don't share real-time data — average coordination delay: 12+ minutes
🚗 Traffic Chaos
No intelligent rerouting causes congestion to cascade for hours
⚠️ Hidden Risks
Roadside safety and environmental risks stay unclear until manual inspection
📉 No Learning
Incident data is siloed — patterns repeat, no prevention insights

✅ Our Solution — Multi-Agent Architecture

S.I.R.S deploys 6 specialized agents that analyze the incident simultaneously and share insights with a Coordinator Agent that generates the final response plan.
